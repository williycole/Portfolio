About

Hi! My name is William Cole Boren, I go by my middle name Cole, and I am a Front-end Developer based out of Southaven, Mississippi. I enjoy solving problems and creating things, I especially enjoy creating things via making websites, applications, games, and anything in between. My goal is to eventually become a full stack developer with an eye for design, and create pristine, pixel perfect, full stack applications. I earned a Bachelors of Science from the University of Mississippi in 2018. After college I did a variety of things for work such as geophysical surveying, concrete and soil testing, coordinating a geotechnical drill crew, making safety of navigation maps for the Department of Defense and much more! In all the jobs I have had I found three things that I really enjoyed in my work. I loved working with a enthusiastic, dedicated, helpful, and friendly team of people. I really enjoy making things, and I love helping people. More specifically I love helping people solve problems. Shortly after learning these things about my work style I found coding, decided to make the switch from an augur slinging, map making Geologist to Frontend Web Development, and the rest is history!
